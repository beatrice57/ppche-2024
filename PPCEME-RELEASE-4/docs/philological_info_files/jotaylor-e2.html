Remarks Taylor compiled three earlier volumes into a single one, maintaining the original pagination. For clarity, each page reference in the corpus is unique, explicitly indicating the appropriate volume. For instance, the three instances of p. 1 in the edition are differentiated as p. 1,1, p. 2,1, and p. 3,1.
The pagination is incorrect in places, but not in any of the corpus samples.
