The translators of the Second Oxford Company were Thomas Ravis (director) (1560-1609), George Abott (1562-1633), Richard Eedes (1555-1604), Giles Tomson (1553-1612), Sir Henry Savile (1549-1622), John Peryn (or Perne) (?-1615), Ralph Ravens (?-1615), John Harmar (?-1613), Leonard Hutten (1557?-1632), John Aglionby (1566-1609), and James Mountague (or Montagu) (1568?-1618) (source: Adam Nicolson. 2003. God's secretaries. HarperCollins. 256-258).
