"Anne, wife of Robert Montague, 3rd Earl of Manchester, and, after his death in 1682, of Charles, Earl of Halifax. She was the daughter of Sir Christopher Yelverton, Bart., of Easton Mauduit, co. Northampton, and was thus the aunt of Lady [Frances] Hatton" (fhatton-1677-e3) (Thompson 1878, vol. 1, 174, fn. e).
