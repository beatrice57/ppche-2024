Remarks

cmlamb1-m1 contains homilies vii-viii and xiv-xvii. Homilies i-v and ix-xiii are contained in cmlambx1-mx1. Homily vi (rhymed) is not included in the corpus.

See Lambeth Homilies (MX1) for further information.
