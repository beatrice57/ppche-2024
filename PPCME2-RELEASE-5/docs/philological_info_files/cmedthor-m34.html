Remarks

This is a northern version of a translation of the Latin Speculum S. Edmundi by Edmund Rich, Archbishop of Canterbury, at Pontigny, around 1350, possibly from a Southern exemplar. The Mirror of St. Edmund (Vernon ms.) is another version of this text.

This text is not included in the Helsinki Corpus.
